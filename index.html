Harvesting Locations

Mr Bigs Puddle

They occupy a small spring pool in Rio Teuchitlan, Mexico.

Fun Fact: Half of it's water supply is tequila.

Old Gregg's Swamp

These fish live in waters near New Zealand and Australia as well as the Atlantic coast of Europe and Africa. The species is also common in the Mediterranean and the Black Sea.

Fun Fact:Old Greg doesn't like people in his waters, but does enjoy drinking Bailey's from shoes.

Harolds Home

The Anglerfish live in the deep-sea regions of the Pacific Ocean, the Indian Ocean, the Atlantic Ocean, the Arctic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea.

Fun Fact: Harold has a pretty face, and a heart of gold.

Lil' Guy's Grotto

The European Sea Sturgeon living in the wild remain in the Garonne River in France. This critically endangered species once inhabited a vast area but no longer do.

Fun Fact: Lil' Guy really isn't that little.

Jazzy's Hand Habitat

Hobarts Frederick Henry Bay off the coast of eastern Tasmania.

Fun Fact: Jazzy actually has the fastest hands in the east.
